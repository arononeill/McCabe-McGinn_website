About Us

Formed in 2012, McCabe McGinn Construction & Management (MCMG) specialise in fit-out within the retail, leisure and office sectors. Offering a comprehensive service, MCMG bring together all the neccessary components to deliver complex projects in a fast-track environment. We strive to deliver an innovative and hands-on approach to all our projects. Providing the neccessary knowledge, experience and resources at an accessible and personalised level, we ensure that our clients' needs and expectations are met to a consistently high level and professional standard.

McCabe McGinn has grown steadily since inception and is well established in both the UK and Irish markets. This knowledge has underpinned the development of a diverse and impressive portfolio of fit-outs from clients including:

Brown Thomas, Arnotts, Cineworld, Superdry, Value Retail, Dunnes Stores, Hermes, Prada, Louis Vuitton, Jimmy Choo, Valentino, HUDA, Selina, Christian Louboutin, Kennedy Wilson, Thom Sweeney, Multi, UCD, G4s, Kildare Village, Ralph Lauren, Mulberry Celine, Tumi, Aura, and Cartier.

Our Services

Our activities consist primarily of fit-out within the retail, leisure, hospitality and office sectors, bringing together all the construction, MEP services and finishes required for a successful project. We are accomplished at working under strict site conditions, with extensive experience in maintaining fully operational construction sites in close proximity to both the client and the public - many within live operational/trading environments.

We have developed a solid supply chain based on service, quality and commitment to reliability and teamwork. Through this supply chain, our range of services extends to the design, production and installation of bespoke joinery, metalwork and glazing to provide the finishing touches to any development. We can offer a full design and build service, managing consultants and contractors to provide a turnkey product.

We have an accreditation of ISO 9001, ISO 14001 and ISO 45001.
